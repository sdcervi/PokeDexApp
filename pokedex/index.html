Thank you all for your feedback on the poll I sent out via email a few days ago!

There was a significant majority of votes in favor of just moving things to a News section on the website, so that’s what I’ve done. You can see news posts now at https://pokedexapp.net/news/, and it's also in the navigation menu at the top of this page too.

Going forward, the newsletter will be powered by Substack. All posts will show up publicly on the News page on the website, and if you’d like to subscribe via email or subscribe via RSS, you can do that, too.

Bonus feature: Substack allows for comments and discussion on posts! This way, it’ll be easier for you to ask any questions, or even chat with each other if you’d like. Enjoy!

All further communications will be via Substack from here on out. I’m not automatically subscribing anyone to Substack; that is opt-in only, and will still be publicly visible on the News page for anyone who doesn’t want to subscribe.

–Your friendly neighborhood web designer, Stephanie

How do I use this?

Toggle between List and Grid view

You can either use the default grid view, where Pokémon are arranged in boxes just like in Pokémon Home, or the list view where each Pokémon and all its variants are on the same row of a list. Use the buttons in the upper left to switch between views.

Alt forms checklist

Each dex type (normal or shiny) also has an alt/variants section. For Alcremie’s alt forms (and others, coming soon), you can use the Alt Forms Guide page to help you track which alt forms you need and how to get the remaining forms. Please note that this does not retain progress like the PokéDex page does.
